program: (block)*
block: func | ife | ifne | whilee | whilene | statement_list
func: FID LBR formparams RBR LFBR statement_list RFBR
ife: IFE LBR actvar RBR LFBR statement_list RFBR
ifne: IFNE LBR actvar RBR LFBR statement_list RFBR
whilee: WHIE LBR actvar RBR LFBR statement_list RFBR
whilene: WHINE LBR actvar RBR LFBR statement_list RFBR
formparams: (formvar COMMA)* formvar | EMP
actvar: ACTVID
formvar: FORMVID
statement_list: (statement)*
statement: assign SEMICOL | funcall SEMICOL | LCOMM COMMENT RCOMM | EMP
assign: var ASSIGN expr
funcall: FID LBR actparams RBR
actparams: (expr COMMA)* expr | EMP
expr: (expr | term) (UNION | INTER | DIFF | SYMDIFF) (expr | term)
term: funcall | actvar | factor | LBR expr RBR
factor: SET
